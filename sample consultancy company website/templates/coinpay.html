Consumer Module

Wallet: Users can easily create and manage digital wallets within the application to securely store and manage their tokens or digital currencies. These Wallets support public and private key encryption and offer features like balance tracking, transaction history, and backup and recovery options.

Token: Enabled creation and management of tokens within the application, including defining token supply, distribution, and smart contract functionality.

Registration: Seamless user registration process and implemented robust authentication mechanisms, such as username/password, biometrics, or two-factor authentication (2FA), to ensure secure access to the application.

Storage: Strong encryption and security measures will take care to safeguard users' tokens and digital assets stored within their wallets. This includes secure storage mechanisms, encryption of sensitive data, and protection against unauthorized access or fraudulent activities.

Exchange: Users can convert or exchange different tokens or digital currencies within the application, with flexibility in managing their funds.

Multi-Device: Accessible across multiple platforms, including web, mobile (iOS and Android), and desktop, to cater to a wide range of users.

P2P Transfers: Seamless peer-to-peer transfers of tokens are facilitated between the users to send and receive funds easily.
